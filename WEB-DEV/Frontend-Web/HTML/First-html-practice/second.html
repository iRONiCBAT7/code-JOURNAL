Arc Shot

A shot in which the subject is photographed by an encircling or moving camera. There is the camera angle is important. How the light come in the lense of the camera. Camera lense should be good. Because the quality of image depend on the lense and the camera sensor



Interlude

A brief, intervening film scene or sequence, not specifically tied to the plot, that appears within a film





Resolution

It depict the no. of pixel in a given image and screen display. Bigger Resolution means bigger image. But Resolution have no any relationship with quality of image




Prologue

A speech, preface, introduction, or brief scene preceding the the main action or plot of a film; contrast to epilogue
